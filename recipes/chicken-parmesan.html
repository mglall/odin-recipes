Chicken Parmesan

Description

the best chicken parmesan with a deliciously crispy
coating, smothered in a rich homemade tomato sauce
and melted mozzarella cheese!
Oven baked Chicken Parmesan is simple to make and worth every minute! If you
love a crispy crumb coating vs a soggy crumb, look no further! The best you will
ever make!

ingredients

  • 4 skinless, boneless chicken breast halves
  • salt and freshly ground black pepper to taste
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 cup panko bread crumbs, or more as needed
  • ¾ cup grated Parmesan cheese, divided
  • 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour, or more if needed
  • ½ cup olive oil for frying, or as needed
  • ½ cup prepared tomato sauce
  • ¼ cup fresh mozzarella, cut into small cubes

Steps

  1. step 1

    Preheat an oven to 450 degrees F (230 degrees C).

  2. step 2

    Place chicken breasts between two sheets of heavy plastic (resealable freezer bags
    work well) on a solid, level surface. Firmly pound chicken with the smooth side of
    a meat mallet to a thickness of 1/2-inch. Season chicken thoroughly with salt and
    pepper.

  3. step 3

    Beat eggs in a shallow bowl and set aside.

  4. step 4

    Mix bread crumbs and 1/2 cup Parmesan cheese in a separate bowl, set aside.

  5. step 5

    Place flour in a sifter or strainer; sprinkle over chicken breasts, evenly coating both
    sides.

  6. step 6

    Dip a flour-coated chicken breast in beaten eggs. Transfer breast to the bread crumb mixture, pressing crumbs
    into both sides. Repeat for each breast. Let chicken rest for
    10 to 15 minutes.
